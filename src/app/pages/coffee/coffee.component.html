Coffee Recipes

Your ultimate guide to brewing perfect coffee!

Espresso

A strong black coffee brewed by forcing steam through ground coffee beans.

Ingredients:

  • Freshly ground coffee
  • Hot water

Latte

A creamy coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk.

Ingredients:

  • Espresso
  • Steamed milk
  • Foamed milk

Cappuccino

A balanced coffee drink with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and foam.

Ingredients:

  • Espresso
  • Steamed milk
  • Milk foam
